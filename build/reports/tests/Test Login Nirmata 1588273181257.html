Nirmata is a turn-key solution that helps enterprises operationalize Kubernetes, an open-source system for automating deployment, scaling, and management of containerized applications. Nirmata provides enterprise IT operations unified management of clusters and workloads, and allows development teams to innovate faster. The Nirmata platform is designed for enterprise needs. Nirmata supports open container technologies and 100% upstream OSS Kubernetes for scalable and efficient application management on any public or private clouds, and even bare-metal servers.

Nirmata is a Kubernetes-based application management platform that lets you easily deploy, operate, and optimize Kubernetes workloads across multiple clusters and clouds. Nirmata is designed to be flexible and adapts to your needs. You can install new Kubernetes clusters using Nirmata, or even import existing clusters, to provide unified visibility and management.
